About Frequency Lab

Pioneering Technology Education in Bangladesh

Frequency Lab is a Bangladesh-based education technology (EdTech) company and social enterprise dedicated to nurturing a new generation of technology innovators and developers.

As the first of its kind in Bangladesh, Frequency Lab empowers children and youth through Coding, Electronics, and Robotics education, helping bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application.

Our Mission

To inspire and equip young minds with essential technology skills for a sustainable and innovative future.

Our Vision

To create a generation of critical thinkers, problem solvers, and technology leaders who can drive positive change globally.

Our Key Focus Areas

Coding

Learn programming fundamentals and build real-world applications through hands-on projects and practical experience.

Electronics

Master circuit design and electronic systems development with comprehensive training in hardware and embedded systems.

Robotics

Create intelligent robots and explore automation technology by combining programming, electronics, and mechanical design.
